Question 1:

Which statement below is correct regarding the difference between a nap and a microsleep?

They are the same thing with different names
A nap lasts for between one and two hours and a microsleep lasts for less than one hour

Incorrect

Question 6: Sleep disorders affect pilot performance. Select all of the options below that describe how sleep deprivation can affect pilot performance:
Lack of concentration
Fatigue
Increased errors
Longer reaction times
Forgetfulness
Correct!
Incorrect
Answer: All of the options describe how pilot performance can be affected by sleep deprivation.
