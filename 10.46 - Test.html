SHORT TEST: SHORT TEST 10.46

The following Short Test is designed to help you review the course material.

Your answers will generate instant feedback - you can redo the question if it is incorrect.

The results from this test will not contribute to your FINAL EXAM score

PPL 10.46
Question 1: Loss of situational awareness can lead to: Choose all correct answers:
airspace infringement
controlled flight into terrain
dangerous weather conditions
loss of aircraft control
Fuel starvation
Correct!
Incorrect
Answer: All options are correct

Question 2: Factors which might interfere with situational awareness are:
weather patterns
interpersonal differences
hopes, wishes and desires
poor instrument layouts
Correct!
Incorrect
Answer: hopes, wishes and desires

Question 3: What are the three Levels of Situational Awareness?
ASSESS, TAKE ACTION, EVALUATE
PERCEIVE, COMPREHEND, PROJECT
ANTICIPATE, ACTION, PROJECT
OBSERVE, ASSESS, ANTICIPATE
Correct!
Incorrect
Answer: PERCEIVE, COMPREHEND, PROJECT

Question 4: Situational awareness is: 
the process that ensures the pilot maintains an accurate model of his/her environment
the process that ensures the pilot maintains an accurate model of the situation within the cockpit
the process that ensures the pilot maintains an accurate model of the situation outside the cockpit
the process that ensures the pilot maintains an accurate model of the situation on and around the landing point
Correct!
Incorrect
Answer: the process that ensures the pilot maintains an accurate model of his/her environment

Question 5: Which of the following key points below are recommended to maintain and enhance Situational Awareness when flying? Choose all of the correct options
Communicate - Keep all crewmembers and external participants in the loop
Manage attention and workload - Set priorities, avoid distraction
Seek information - Use your senses
Focus on one item at a time
Brief others on what you expect -Compare actual state with expectations and objectives Readjust your plan if required
Correct!
Incorrect
Answer: All but focusing on one item are good for SA. Do not fixate on any one thing to the exclusion of everything else
